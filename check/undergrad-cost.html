ESTIMATED COSTS AND FINANCIAL AID

LIUS offers a world-class education and competitive tuition rates in one of Canada's most affordable urban centres. Your tuition and fees will depend on your program.

Note 1: Purchase of a meal plan is required for students residing in Sherbrooke and optional for those residing in other residences. Food costs for students living may vary. Cost is estimated based on Statistics Canada data.

Note 2: A student public transit pass for September to April is included in LIUS’s non- instructional fees. From May to August, students may purchase a public transit pass.
